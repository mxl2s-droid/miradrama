# Design Notes

The visual direction follows the supplied Together AI reference:

- Near-black hero and compliance bands.
- Bright white content bands.
- One orange to magenta to periwinkle gradient accent.
- Compact uppercase mono labels for buttons and eyebrows.
- Flat cards with 4px radius and hairline borders.
- No heavy shadows or decorative background clutter.
- Large pale footer wordmark as the final brand signal.
